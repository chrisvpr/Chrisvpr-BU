Hello .

With a natural talent for empathy building and passion for technology, I started my journey with the idea that I can change how the word is design for the better. My goal is to place the user at the center of every experience, adding value to their lives.

I worked as a User Experience Designer for several years, idealizing my creative problem-solving skills using research and data. Also, I used to collaborate on open-source projects, the last one being Erabo App .

Today, as the Director of User Experience at Peña Defilló, I run a small multidisciplinary team called Digitas, that designs, builds, and scale digital products.
